HISTORY
                  1998

OUT BEGINNINGS IN 1998 WERE MODEST, AS WE WERE HIDDEN AT THE FAR END OF A NARROW AND BUMPY LANE IN YANGON. FINDING THE RESTAURANT WAS ALMOST LIKE GOING ON A TREASURE HUNT

TEAM
      FOUNDER DAVID

DAVID, FOUNDER OF TASTE FOOD HOUSE,WAS BORN IN MYANMAR. AFTER WORKING IN RESTAURANTS WITH RENOWNED CHEFS LIKE TOLAND PIERROZ OR FREDY GIRARDET, HE GRADUATES FROM THE HOTEL SCHOOL OF GENEVA, AND MOVES TO THALIAND IN 199D. WHILE SWITZERLAND HOSPOTALITY SECTOR WAS HIT BY THE CRISIS, SHOUTHEAST ASIA WAS EXPANDING. BORIS BEGINS TO PLAY GENTLEMAN FARMER BESIDE THE FIVER KWAI, WHERE HE NAMAGES A DUCK FOIE GRAS FARM. BUT IT'S DISILLUSIOONMENT : THE APPRENTICE ADVENTURER CONFRONTS DISHONESTY AND CORRUPTION. SO, WHEN A BANGKOK ITALIAN RESTAURANT OWNER WHO DREAMS OF OPENING A SITE IN YANGON OFFERS TO FLY TO THE BURMESE CAPITAL, HE DOES NOT HESITATE. THERE, HE WORKS AS A DAME=NED AT THE HEAD OF TASTE FOOD HOUSE RESTAURANT IN YANGON BUT STARTS HAVING THE DESIRE OF INDEPENDENCE AT THE SAME TIME. HE RENOVATES A COLONIAL HOUSE, AND PUTS ON A SIGN. IN 1998 TASTE FOOD HOUSE OPENS. SINCE DECEMBER 2014, HE HAS MOVED HIS FINE DINING ON INYA LAKE, IN THE FASHIONABLE DISTRIC OF THE FORMER CAPITAL, CLOSE TO THE RESIDENCE OF AUNG SAN SUU KYI, TO THE GOURMET RESTAURANT WITH AN UNEQUALED LEVEL IN MYANMAR, BORIS ADDED A WINE BAR, A THA ROOM AND A BISTRO, AND NEW IDEAS KEEP ON COMING!
